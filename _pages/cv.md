## Professional Summary

I am a researcher and engineer working at the intersection of **human-robot interaction, perception, and deployable robot systems**. I build collaborative robotic experiences end to end—from experiment design and sensing pipelines to robot behavior implementation and analysis.

What I bring:

- **Research depth with systems execution:** publications in ACM/IEEE HRI, plus hands-on robot deployment and public demos.
- **Strong technical breadth:** ROS, computer vision, gaze processing, machine learning, Python, experimental tooling, and data analysis.
- **Human-centered evaluation skills:** user studies, multimodal behavioral data, and statistical modeling for HRI.
